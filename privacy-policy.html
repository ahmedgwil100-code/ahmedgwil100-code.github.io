Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Introduction

ETYN Tools ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website and tools.

Information We Collect

Data You Enter Into Tools

All data you enter into our tools (text, numbers, images, files) is processed entirely within your web browser using client-side JavaScript. This data is never transmitted to our servers, stored in any database, or shared with any third party. When you close or refresh the page, all data is discarded.

Analytics and Cookies

We may use analytics services (such as Google Analytics) to understand how visitors use our website. These services may collect anonymized data such as page views, device type, browser type, and approximate location. This data is used solely to improve our website and is not linked to any personally identifiable information.

Advertising

Our website may display advertisements through Google AdSense or similar advertising networks. These services may use cookies to serve ads based on your browsing history. You can manage your ad preferences through Google's Ad Settings or opt out through the Digital Advertising Alliance's opt-out page.

localStorage

We use browser localStorage to save your theme preference (light or dark mode). This data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect personal information (name, email, address) unless you voluntarily submit it through our contact form
  • We do not require user accounts or sign-ups
  • We do not store files you upload to our tools
  • We do not sell or share any user data with third parties

Third-Party Services

Our website may include links to third-party websites and services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our Contact page.
