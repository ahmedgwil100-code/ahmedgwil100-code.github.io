How It Works

This tool uses color-based background removal. It analyzes each pixel in your image and makes pixels transparent if their color is within the specified tolerance of the selected background color. Works best with solid-color backgrounds (white, green, blue, etc.).

Tips for Best Results

  • Use images with solid or near-solid backgrounds
  • Click on the background area of your image to auto-detect the color
