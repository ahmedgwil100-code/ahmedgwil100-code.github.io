How It Works

This tool uses the Web Speech Synthesis API to convert your text into spoken audio. When you click "Speak & Record Audio", it captures the audio output through the MediaRecorder API so you can download it.

Tips

  • Different browsers offer different voices ‚Äî Chrome has the most
  • Adjust speed for faster/slower narration
  • Adjust pitch for higher/lower voice tones
  • The "Speak & Record" feature requires your browser to support audio capture

FAQ

This converter version adds downloadable audio recording, more voice options, and fine-tuned speed and pitch controls.

The audio is recorded in WebM format (or WAV depending on browser), which is compatible with most media players.
