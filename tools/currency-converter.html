How It Works

This currency converter fetches real-time exchange rates from the free ExchangeRate-API service. Simply enter an amount, select your currencies, and click Convert. The tool supports 30+ major global currencies including USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, TND, SAR, AED, and many more.

Supported Currencies

  • Americas: USD (US Dollar), CAD (Canadian Dollar), BRL (Brazilian Real), MXN (Mexican Peso)
  • Europe: EUR (Euro), GBP (British Pound), CHF (Swiss Franc), SEK (Swedish Krona), NOK (Norwegian Krone)
  • Asia-Pacific: JPY (Japanese Yen), CNY (Chinese Yuan), INR (Indian Rupee), KRW (South Korean Won), AUD (Australian Dollar), NZD (New Zealand Dollar), SGD (Singapore Dollar)
  • Middle East & Africa: AED (UAE Dirham), SAR (Saudi Riyal), TND (Tunisian Dinar), QAR (Qatari Riyal), EGP (Egyptian Pound), ZAR (South African Rand), MAD (Moroccan Dirham)

Accuracy
